# Power Requirements

## STM32 NUCLEO-G071RB

- Supply Voltage: 5V (USB)
- Operating Voltage: 3.3V

---

## HC-SR04

- Operating Voltage: 5V
- Current Consumption: <15mA

---

## L298N

- Motor Supply: 7V–12V
- Logic Supply: 5V

---

## DC Motor

- Rated Voltage: 12V

---

## Total System

| Module | Supply |
|---------|--------|
| STM32 | USB 5V |
| HC-SR04 | 5V |
| L298N | 12V |
| DC Motor | 12V |

Ensure that all modules share a common ground to maintain reliable signal communication.
